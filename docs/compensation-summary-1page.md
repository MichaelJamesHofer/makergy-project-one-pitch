# PeptideTracker Compensation Structure - Executive Summary

## Current Situation
**Work Completed**: Technical Founder (95%) | Business Co-founder (5%) | Marketing (0%)
**Funding**: $1M seed round | **Required Runway**: 24 months | **Team Size**: 5-7 people by EOY

## Proposed Compensation Structure

### Base Salaries (Monthly)
| Role | Months 1-3 | Months 4-6 | Months 7-12 | Market Rate |
|------|------------|------------|-------------|-------------|
| **CEO/CTO** (You) | $8,000 | $8,000 | $8,000 | $10,000 |
| **COO** (Co-founder) | $5,000 | $6,000* | $7,000* | $8,500 |
| **Sr. Developer** | - | $7,000 | $7,000 | $10,000 |
| **Marketing Lead** | - | $4,000 | $4,000 | $6,000 |

*Increases tied to achieving specific milestones

### COO Milestone Gates
**$5,000 → $6,000**: 3+ vendor partnerships + affiliate program launch
**$6,000 → $7,000**: $100k partnership revenue + 15% conversion rate  
**$7,000 → $8,500**: Series A metrics + break-even operations

## Financial Impact

### Runway Analysis
- **Proposed Structure**: 24+ months runway, break-even by month 9
- **Equal Draws ($8k each)**: 18 months runway, break-even by month 12
- **Difference**: 6 additional months of operation

### Year 1 Budget Allocation ($1M)
- Personnel (55%): $550k
- Marketing (20%): $200k  
- Infrastructure (15%): $150k
- Operations (10%): $100k

## Key Rationale

1. **Merit-Based**: Compensation reflects actual contribution and value creation
2. **Sustainable**: Preserves runway while building team
3. **Incentive-Aligned**: Clear milestones drive specific outcomes
4. **Market-Competitive**: Below market initially, normalizes with success
5. **Investor-Friendly**: Shows fiscal discipline and performance focus

## Comparable Companies
Successful SaaS startups typically started with differentiated founder compensation:
- **Calendly**: Tech $65k / Biz $45k → $3B valuation
- **Notion**: Tech $70k / Biz $50k → $10B valuation
- **Airtable**: Tech $80k / Biz $60k → $11B valuation

## Bottom Line
This structure acknowledges past contributions while incentivizing future performance, extends runway by 6 months, and aligns with successful startup precedents. Compensation equalizes at Series A or when company achieves sustainable profitability.
