## Quick Scoring Matrix

### Step 1: Calculate Base Scores

#### Technical Score (Max 100 points)
```
Technical Skills (B1):
- Each "Can Do Independently" = 3 points (30 max)
- Each "Need Help" = 1 point
- "Could build alone" Yes = 20 points
- Technical hours logged (A1): 
  - 1000+ hours = 30 points
  - 500-999 hours = 20 points
  - 100-499 hours = 10 points
  - <100 hours = 5 points
- Technical outputs created (A1):
  - Each checked = 3 points (20 max)

TECHNICAL TOTAL: _____ / 100
```

#### Business Score (Max 100 points)
```
Business Skills (B2):
- Each "Can Do Independently" = 3 points (30 max)
- Each "Need Help" = 1 point
- "Could run business alone" Yes = 20 points
- Business hours logged (A1):
  - 500+ hours = 30 points
  - 250-499 hours = 20 points
  - 100-249 hours = 10 points
  - <100 hours = 5 points
- Business outputs created (A1):
  - Each checked = 3 points (20 max)

BUSINESS TOTAL: _____ / 100
```

#### Commitment Score (Max 100 points)
```
Time Availability (C1):
- 50+ hrs/week = 30 points
- 40-49 hrs/week = 25 points
- 30-39 hrs/week = 15 points
- 20-29 hrs/week = 10 points
- <20 hrs/week = 5 points

Financial Runway (C2):
- 12+ months = 20 points
- 6-11 months = 15 points
- 3-5 months = 10 points
- <3 months = 5 points

Long-term Commitment (C3):
- 3+ years = 30 points
- 2 years = 20 points
- 1 year = 10 points
- <1 year = 5 points

Value Creation (D1-D3):
- Each deliverable checked = 2 points (20 max)

COMMITMENT TOTAL: _____ / 100
```

## Step 2: Role Determination

### CEO Indicators
**Required**: Technical Score >70 OR Business Score >70, AND Commitment Score >80

**Strong Indicators:**
- ‚úì Made major decisions independently (A3)
- ‚úì Can work 50+ hours/week
- ‚úì Long-term commitment (3+ years)
- ‚úì High risk tolerance
- ‚úì Leadership experience (managed 5+ people)
- ‚úì "Company dies if I leave" (D3)
- ‚úì Willing to be measured on overall company metrics

**Red Flags:**
- ‚úó Looking for other jobs
- ‚úó <40 hours/week available
- ‚úó Cannot handle ambiguity
- ‚úó Avoids difficult conversations
- ‚úó 50/50 or less chance of being here in 1 year

### COO Indicators
**Required**: Business Score >60 AND Commitment Score >70

**Strong Indicators:**
- ‚úì Strong at partnerships (B2)
- ‚úì Can generate revenue (D2)
- ‚úì Operational experience
- ‚úì Good at execution
- ‚úì Comfortable with clear metrics
- ‚úì 40+ hours/week available

**Red Flags:**
- ‚úó No revenue generation ability
- ‚úó Poor at negotiations
- ‚úó Cannot manage P&L
- ‚úó Limited availability

### CTO Indicators
**Required**: Technical Score >80 AND Commitment Score >70

**Strong Indicators:**
- ‚úì Can build independently (B1)
- ‚úì 1000+ technical hours logged
- ‚úì Created core technical assets
- ‚úì Can do code reviews
- ‚úì Architecture experience
- ‚úì 40+ hours/week for coding

**Red Flags:**
- ‚úó Needs help with basic tasks
- ‚úó Cannot deploy to production
- ‚úó No security knowledge
- ‚úó Limited coding time

### Advisory/Part-Time Indicators
**Indicated when**: All scores <60 OR Commitment Score <50

**Characteristics:**
- Limited availability (<20 hrs/week)
- Other primary commitments
- Specific expertise only
- Short-term commitment
- Looking for other opportunities

## Step 3: Compensation Level Determination

### Compensation Formula
```
Base Rate = (Role Score √ó $100) + (Commitment Score √ó $50) + (Value Score √ó $30)

Adjustments:
- Past contribution 80%+ = +$2,000
- Past contribution 50-79% = +$1,000
- Past contribution 20-49% = +$500
- Financial pressure >7 = +$500
- Other income available = -$1,000
- Part-time only = -50%
```

### Compensation Bands

#### Tier 1: $8,000-10,000/month
- CEO role qualified
- 90+ overall score
- Full-time commitment
- Critical to success
- Major past contribution

#### Tier 2: $6,000-7,999/month
- COO/CTO qualified
- 75-89 overall score
- Full-time available
- Important contributor
- Milestone increases available

#### Tier 3: $4,000-5,999/month
- Department head level
- 60-74 overall score
- 30+ hours/week
- Growing into role
- Performance-based increases

#### Tier 4: $2,000-3,999/month
- Part-time contributor
- 45-59 overall score
- 20-30 hours/week
- Limited experience
- Learning opportunity

#### Tier 5: Equity Only
- Advisory role
- <45 overall score
- <20 hours/week
- Minimal commitment
- Other primary income

## Step 5: Equity Adjustment Recommendations

### Equity Formula
```
Base Equity = (Past Contribution % √ó 0.5) + (Future Value % √ó 0.5)

Where:
- Past Contribution = Hours worked / Total hours √ó Technical outputs / Total outputs
- Future Value = (Role Score + Commitment Score) / 200

Adjustments:
- Below market salary = +2-5% equity
- Part-time only = -50% equity
- Short-term commitment = -30% equity
- Critical skills = +2-3% equity
```

### Equity Bands

**Founder Level (20-50%)**
- Major past contribution (>70%)
- Full-time commitment
- Critical role (CEO/CTO)
- Long-term commitment

**Executive Level (5-20%)**
- Moderate contribution (30-70%)
- Full-time or near full-time
- Important role (COO/VP)
- 2+ year commitment

**Early Employee (1-5%)**
- Limited contribution (<30%)
- Full-time hire
- Department head
- Standard vesting

**Advisory (0.1-1%)**
- Minimal contribution
- Part-time/advisory only
- Specific expertise
- Short-term involvement

## Step 7: Difficult Conversation Guide

### If Recommending Lower Role/Compensation Than Expected

**Opening Framework:**
"Based on the assessment, the data suggests [role] at [compensation] best aligns with your current contribution and capacity..."

**Data Points to Reference:**
1. Hours invested comparison
2. Tangible outputs created
3. Available time commitment
4. Skill gaps identified
5. Market comparables

**Offering Path Forward:**
"Here's how to reach [desired level]:
- Achieve [specific milestone]
- Develop [specific skill]
- Increase availability to [hours]
- Deliver [specific outcome]"

### If Major Misalignment Found

**Critical Questions to Address:**
1. Is this person essential to success?
2. Can gaps be filled with hires?
3. Is part-time/advisory better?
4. Should equity be restructured?
5. Is separation necessary?

### Recommended Structure Based on Deltas

**Large Delta (>30 points difference):**
- Clear senior/junior dynamic
- Significant compensation difference
- Different vesting schedules
- Consider advisory for lower scorer

**Medium Delta (15-30 points):**
- Complementary roles (CEO/COO)
- Moderate compensation difference
- Milestone-based equalization
- Clear responsibility split

**Small Delta (<15 points):**
- Co-CEO consideration
- Similar compensation
- Equal equity possible
- Shared responsibilities
