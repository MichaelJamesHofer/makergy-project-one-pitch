## Scoring Guide (For Analyst Use)

### Role Fit Scoring

**CEO Material (120+ points)**
- Strong technical OR business background
- 50+ hours/week availability
- High risk tolerance
- Leadership experience
- Long-term commitment

**COO Material (100+ points)**
- Operational excellence
- Partnership focus
- Revenue generation ability
- 40+ hours/week availability
- Execution focused

**CTO Material (100+ points)**
- Deep technical skills
- Can code independently
- Architecture experience
- 40+ hours/week availability
- Product vision

**Advisory Role (<80 points)**
- Limited availability
- Specific expertise
- Low commitment level
- Other priorities
- Part-time only

### Compensation Level Indicators

**Premium Compensation ($8k+)**
- Critical to success
- Irreplaceable skills
- Full-time commitment
- Major value creation
- Proven track record

**Standard Compensation ($5-7k)**
- Important contributor
- Solid skills
- Good availability
- Growth potential
- Some experience

**Below Market (<$5k)**
- Learning on job
- Limited availability
- Unclear value
- No track record
- Other income sources

**Equity Only**
- Minimal time commitment
- Advisory capacity
- Already compensated elsewhere
- Unproven ability
- High risk profile
