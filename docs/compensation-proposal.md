## 2. Organizational Structure & Responsibilities

### C-Suite Roles & Responsibilities

#### Chief Executive Officer / Chief Technology Officer (Technical Founder)
**Monthly Draw**: $8,000 ($96,000 annually)

**Primary Responsibilities**:
- Technical architecture and platform development
- Product strategy and roadmap execution
- Engineering team leadership and hiring
- Investor relations and board reporting
- Technical infrastructure decisions

**Key Performance Indicators**:

- Feature delivery on schedule
- User satisfaction >4.5/5

- Legal and compliance oversight
- Revenue optimization strategies
- Pricing strategy execution
- Partnership negotiations

**Key Performance Indicators**:
- Secure 5+ vendor partnerships by Q2
- Achieve 15% affiliate conversion rate
- Reduce CAC by 20% by year-end
- Establish 3+ strategic partnerships

**Milestone for Compensation Increase**:
- ✓ 3+ signed vendor partnerships
- ✓ $100k in partnership-driven revenue
- ✓ Operational cost reduction of 15%

#### Head of Marketing (To Be Hired Q1)
**Monthly Draw**: $4,000 ($48,000 annually)

**Primary Responsibilities**:
- User acquisition strategy

## 3. Compensation Philosophy

### Guiding Principles

1. **Merit-Based**: Compensation reflects actual contribution and value creation
2. **Performance-Driven**: Increases tied to measurable business outcomes
3. **Market-Competitive**: Aligned with seed-stage SaaS benchmarks
4. **Runway-Conscious**: Maintains 20+ month runway
5. **Equity-Aligned**: Cash compensation balanced with equity incentives

### Benchmark Comparison

| Role | PeptideTracker | Industry Average (Seed) | Variance |
|------|---------------|------------------------|----------|
| Technical CEO | $96k | $100-120k | -15% |
| Business COO | $60-84k | $80-100k | -20% |
| Senior Developer | $84k | $90-120k | -20% |
| Marketing Lead | $48k | $60-80k | -30% |

*Note: Below-market cash compensation should be offset with equity grants*

---

## 4. Equity Considerations

### Recommended Equity Adjustments

Given the 95/5 contribution split to date:

1. **Founder Equity Vesting**
   - Technical Founder: 1-year cliff already satisfied based on work completed
   - Business Co-founder: Standard 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff

2. **Performance Equity Pool** (5% of company)
   - Reserved for milestone achievements
   - Distributed based on KPI attainment

3. **Employee Option Pool** (15% of company)
   - Senior developers: 0.5-1.5%
   - Marketing lead: 0.5-1%
   - Junior developers: 0.25-0.5%

## 7. Compensation Review Triggers

### Automatic Review Events
1. **Revenue Milestones**
   - $100k MRR: 10% salary increase pool
   - $200k MRR: 15% salary increase pool
   - Break-even: 20% salary increase pool

2. **User Growth Milestones**
   - 50k users: Marketing bonus
   - 100k users: Team bonus pool
   - 200k users: Executive bonus

3. **Funding Events**
   - Series A close: Normalize to market rates
   - Strategic acquisition offer: Retention bonuses

### Performance Review Schedule
- **Monthly**: KPI review and adjustment
- **Quarterly**: Formal performance evaluation
- **Annually**: Comprehensive compensation review

## 8. Risk Mitigation Strategies

### Compensation Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Co-founder disputes over compensation | High | Clear milestone-based increases |
| Runway depletion | Critical | Conservative hiring, performance gates |
| Key employee turnover | High | Competitive equity packages |
| Market downturn | Medium | Flexible contractor model |

### Contingency Plans

**If Revenue Below Target**:
- Founders take 20% pay cut first
- Delay non-critical hires
- Increase equity components

**If Ahead of Target**:
- Accelerate hiring plan
- Increase marketing spend
- Implement retention bonuses
